# Uniform and Grooming Standards

## General Requirements

All crew members must present a professional, well-groomed appearance while in uniform. The American Airlines uniform represents our brand and commitment to excellence.

## Pilot Uniforms

### Standard Components
- Navy blue jacket with company insignia
- White dress shirt with epaulettes
- Navy blue trousers
- Black leather belt
- Black leather shoes (polished)
- Navy blue tie or scarf
- Wings pin
- Name badge

### Captain Distinctions
- Four gold stripes on epaulettes and sleeves
- Gold wings pin

### First Officer Distinctions
- Three gold stripes on epaulettes and sleeves
- Silver wings pin

### Optional Items
- Navy blue sweater or vest
- Company-approved overcoat
- Rain-resistant jacket

## Flight Attendant Uniforms

### Female Options
- Navy blue dress
- Navy blue skirt suit
- Navy blue pants suit
- Approved scarf or neckerchief
- Low-heeled pumps (1-2 inches)
- Flesh-toned or navy hosiery

### Male Options
- Navy blue suit
- White dress shirt
- Navy blue tie
- Black leather shoes
- Dark socks

### All Flight Attendants
- Wings pin
- Name badge
- Company-approved watch
- Apron for service

## Grooming Standards

### Hair
- Clean and neatly styled
- Natural colors only
- Men: above collar, neat sideburns
- Women: secured if below shoulders
- Extreme styles not permitted

### Facial Hair (Men)
- Clean-shaven preferred
- Neatly trimmed mustache acceptable
- Beards: must be full, trimmed, well-maintained
- Stubble not acceptable

### Nails
- Clean and well-manicured
- Women: moderate length, conservative colors
- French manicure acceptable
- No nail art or extreme colors

### Makeup (Women)
- Professional and moderate
- Natural-looking preferred
- Bold colors acceptable if tasteful
- False eyelashes: natural-looking only

### Jewelry
- Wedding/engagement rings
- Watch (professional style)
- Small stud earrings (women)
- One small ring per hand
- No visible body piercings
- Religious items may be worn discreetly

### Tattoos
- Must be covered while in uniform
- Makeup or bandages acceptable
- No new visible tattoos

### Fragrance
- Light, subtle scents only
- Consider passenger allergies
- None preferred in cabin

## Uniform Care

### Maintenance
- Clean and pressed at all times
- No stains, tears, or missing buttons
- Shoes polished
- Replace worn items promptly

### Cleaning Allowance
- $30/month uniform maintenance
- Submit receipts for reimbursement
- Dry cleaning recommended

### Replacement
- Normal wear: company-provided replacement
- Lost items: employee cost
- Damaged on duty: company replacement

## Uniform Wearing Guidelines

### When Required
- All duty periods
- Deadhead travel
- Training
- Company events
- Commuting to/from work

### When Prohibited
- Personal activities
- Bars or nightclubs
- Political events
- Social media (without approval)

### Layover Guidelines
- May wear casual clothes at hotel
- Uniform required to/from airport
- Jacket may be removed in crew van

## Seasonal Variations

### Summer
- Short-sleeve shirts permitted
- Lighter weight fabrics
- Jackets optional in some locations

### Winter
- Company-approved outerwear
- Scarves and gloves in navy/black
- Cold weather accessories available

## Special Circumstances

### Maternity
- Maternity uniform available
- Same standards apply
- Comfort accommodations made

### Medical Accommodations
- Orthopedic shoes with approval
- Compression stockings
- Other items with medical documentation

### Religious Accommodations
- Requests considered individually
- Head coverings may be approved
- Must maintain professional appearance

## Ordering Uniforms

### New Hires
- Initial allotment provided
- Fitted at training
- Allow 4-6 weeks delivery

### Replacement Orders
- Through uniform portal
- Annual allowance for Flight Attendants
- As-needed for pilots

### Sizing Changes
- Free replacement for significant changes
- Tailoring reimbursement available

## Violations

### First Offense
- Verbal counseling
- Correct immediately

### Repeated Violations
- Written warning
- May affect performance review

### Serious Violations
- Removal from trip
- Disciplinary action
