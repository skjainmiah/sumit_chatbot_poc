# Crew Training and Recurrency Requirements

## Training Categories

### Initial Training
Required for all new hires before line operations:
- Ground school: 4-6 weeks
- Simulator training: 20-40 hours
- Line operating experience: 25 hours minimum
- Check ride: Oral and practical examination

### Recurrent Training
Required annually for all crew members:
- Ground school refresher: 8 hours
- Simulator proficiency check: 4 hours
- Emergency procedures review: 4 hours
- CRM training: 8 hours

### Type Rating Training
Required when transitioning to a new aircraft type:
- Ground school: 2-4 weeks
- Simulator training: 40+ hours
- Line qualification: 20-50 hours
- Type rating check ride

## Proficiency Check Requirements
- Conducted in full-flight simulator
- Must demonstrate:
  - Normal operations
  - Abnormal procedures
  - Emergency procedures
  - Instrument approaches
- Passing score: 80% or higher
- Failed check: Retraining required

## Training Failure Policy
1. First failure: Additional training session
2. Second failure: Extended training program
3. Third failure: Review board evaluation
4. Training records retained for 5 years

## CRM (Crew Resource Management)
Annual CRM training covers:
- Communication
- Situational awareness
- Decision making
- Workload management
- Team building
- Threat and error management

## Emergency Training
Annual emergency drill requirements:
- Evacuation procedures (land and water)
- Fire fighting
- Decompression response
- First aid
- Security threats
- Use of emergency equipment

## Training Centers
American Airlines operates training facilities at:
- DFW - Main training center
- ORD - Regional facility
- MIA - Regional facility
- CLT - Regional facility

## Scheduling Training
- Training is scheduled by Training Administration
- Minimum 30 days notice for recurrent training
- Crew members may request specific dates
- Training takes priority over line flying

## Records and Certificates
- All certificates stored in CrewPortal
- Crew responsible for monitoring expiry dates
- 90-day reminder notifications sent automatically
- Operating with expired certificates is prohibited
