# Crew Leave and Absence Management Policy

## Annual Leave Entitlement

### By Seniority
| Years of Service | Annual Leave Days |
|------------------|-------------------|
| 0-2 years        | 15 days           |
| 3-5 years        | 20 days           |
| 6-10 years       | 25 days           |
| 10+ years        | 30 days           |

## Leave Bidding Process
1. Annual leave is bid during the October bid period
2. Priority is based on seniority number
3. Maximum of 2 weeks may be bid at one time
4. Remaining days can be used as floating days

## Sick Leave
- All crew members receive 12 sick days per year
- Medical certificate required after 3 consecutive days
- Sick leave cannot be carried over
- Pattern sick leave may require medical verification

## Maternity Leave
- 16 weeks total leave available
- 8 weeks mandatory post-delivery
- May combine with annual leave for extended absence
- Flight status: Grounded at 26 weeks pregnancy

## Paternity Leave
- 4 weeks paid leave within 12 months of birth/adoption
- Can be taken consecutively or split into 2-week blocks
- Must notify HR at least 30 days in advance

## Emergency Leave
- 5 days per year for immediate family emergencies
- Immediate family: spouse, children, parents, siblings
- Documentation may be required
- Does not count against annual leave

## Leave Carry-Over Rules
- Maximum 5 days may be carried to next year
- Carried days must be used by March 31
- No cash-out option for unused leave

## Leave Cancellation
- Company may recall crew from leave for operational needs
- 48-hour notice required for recall
- Recalled days are restored to balance
- Premium pay applies to recalled days

## Unpaid Leave of Absence
- Available for personal reasons
- Maximum 6 months
- Benefits continue if employee pays premiums
- Seniority continues to accrue
- Subject to management approval

## Jury Duty
- Paid leave for jury service
- Must provide court documentation
- Available for duty during non-court hours

## Bereavement Leave
- 3 days for immediate family
- 1 day for extended family
- May be extended with manager approval

## How to Request Leave
1. Submit request in CrewPortal
2. Manager approval required within 7 days
3. Bid leave has priority over floating requests
4. Emergency leave: Call crew scheduling immediately
